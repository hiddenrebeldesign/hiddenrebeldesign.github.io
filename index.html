Bassist/Arranger/Co-Founder

Who Am I?

♦

Hello! My name is Kevin, thank you for stopping by.


Ever since I was young, I've had a passion for making things. This affection for creation began with piano and legos, and has since evolved into an ever-growing list of creative pursuiets. If it requires working with my hands, I'm there. If it makes noise, contains moving parts, or delights the senses, count me in. I'm always on a quest for something new to challenge my mind.

Of all of my interests, the bass has provided me with the most challenges and the greatest rewards. I've had the pleasure of playing many instruments, but it is the bass that resonates with me the most. As one who enjoys supporting others, participating in a team, and a good, physical challenge, the bass is the perfect fit. It has enriched my life in countless ways, led me to fantastic friendships, and taken me across the world.
